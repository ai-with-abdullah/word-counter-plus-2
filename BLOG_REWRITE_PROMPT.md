## PROMPT START

```
I need you to completely rewrite and optimize this blog post for SEO: [PASTE YOUR BLOG URL HERE]

---

## 1. CONTENT ANALYSIS FIRST
- Read the current blog content
- Identify the main topic and target keyword
- Find 5-8 related long-tail keywords that are easier to rank for
- Note what internal links should be added

---

## 2. TITLE & META REQUIREMENTS

**Page Title (H1):**
- Must be EXACTLY the same as what will show in Google SERP
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include primary keyword near the beginning
- Add current year (2025 or 2026) if relevant
- Make it compelling with numbers or power words

**Meta Description:**
- 150-160 characters exactly
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Add a call-to-action ("Learn how", "Discover", "Find out")
- Make it click-worthy

**Example Format:**
Title: "How to Count Words in Your Essay: Complete Guide 2025"
Meta: "Learn how to count words accurately in your essays using free tools. Discover tips for meeting word limits and improving your writing. Try it now!"

---

## 3. CONTENT STRUCTURE (3,000+ words)

Write in this exact structure:

### Introduction (200-300 words)
- Start with a hook or relatable problem
- Use conversational phrases like "Let's dive in", "Here's the thing", "You might be wondering"
- Briefly explain what the reader will learn
- Include primary keyword in first 100 words

### Main Body Sections (2,000-2,500 words)
- Use H2 and H3 headings with keywords
- Write 5-7 main sections
- Each section: 300-400 words
- **Bold the long-tail keywords** slightly more than regular text (not excessively)
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Include bullet points and numbered lists
- Add relevant examples and practical tips

### Conclusion (200-300 words)
- Summarize key takeaways
- Include a soft call-to-action
- End with an engaging question or next step

---

## 4. LONG-TAIL KEYWORDS TO TARGET

Based on Word Counter Plus App's niche, naturally include these types of long-tail keywords throughout the content (bold them slightly):

- **word counter for essays**
- **character count checker online**
- **how to check word count in Google Docs**
- **free word counter tool**
- **paragraph word counter**
- **text analyzer for students**
- **word limit checker for assignments**
- **online character counter free**
- **reading time calculator**
- **sentence counter tool**

Choose 5-8 relevant ones based on the blog topic and sprinkle them naturally throughout.

---

- Link to other related blog posts on the site

---

## 6. WRITING STYLE

**Use humanized, conversational language:**
- "Let's talk about..."
- "Here's what you need to know..."
- "The thing is..."
- "You might be wondering..."
- "Let's break this down..."
- "Here's a quick tip..."
- "Think about it this way..."
- "Let's dive into..."
- "The good news is..."
- "Here's the deal..."

**Avoid:**
- Robotic, formal language
- Overly complex sentences
- Passive voice (use active voice)
- Generic filler content

**Write like you're explaining to a friend, not writing an academic paper.**

---

## 7. FAQ SECTION (MANDATORY)

Add a FAQ section at the end with 7-12 questions. Format exactly like this:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: [Question with long-tail keyword]?
[Answer in 2-4 sentences. Be helpful and concise.]

### Q2: [Question with long-tail keyword]?
[Answer in 2-4 sentences. Be helpful and concise.]

... (continue for 7-12 questions total)

**FAQ Guidelines:**
- Use question-based long-tail keywords
- Answer directly and concisely
- Include internal links where relevant
- These help with featured snippets in Google

---

## 8. DATE & YEAR REFERENCES

- Update all references to current date (use today's date)
- For "best of" or guide posts, use "2025" or "2025-2026"
- Remove any outdated year references (2023, 2024, etc.)
- If discussing future trends, reference 2026

## 9. FINAL OUTPUT FORMAT

Provide the rewritten content in this exact format:

- Title matches what will show in SERP (no mismatch)
- 3,000+ words of quality content
- 5-8 long-tail keywords bolded naturally
- 3-5 internal links to tool pages
- 2-3 internal links to related blog posts
- Conversational, humanized writing style
- 7-12 FAQ questions at the end
- Current date and year references (2025/2026)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Proper H2 and H3 heading structure

---
```
